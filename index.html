Overview

The Emami Robotics Alliance (ERA) is an intercollegiate robotics network designed to connect engineering and robotics clubs across institutions into a shared ecosystem for building and competing in real robotics projects.

Many collegiate robotics clubs operate independently, often with strong student interest but limited access to shared resources, consistent competition formats, or cross-campus collaboration. As a result, technical knowledge, fabrication capacity, and event organization are frequently confined to individual campuses.

ERA provides a structured framework to address these challenges. Through standardized competition formats, shared technical resources, and coordinated intercollegiate events, ERA enables participating clubs to collaborate, compete, and build within a unified ecosystem.

ERA is execution-focused, hands-on, and built to scale beyond a single campus or year.
