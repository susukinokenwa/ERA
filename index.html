00 — Overview

What
is ERA?

The Emami Robotics Alliance (ERA) is a robotics network designed to connect engineering and robotics clubs across institutions into a shared ecosystem for building, competing, and scaling real hardware projects.

ERA provides standardized competition formats, shared technical resources, and coordinated intercollegiate events — enabling clubs to collaborate, compete, and build within a unified ecosystem.

Execution-focused, hands-on, and built to scale beyond a single campus or year.
